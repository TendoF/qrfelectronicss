Frequently Asked Questions

Are your iPhones genuine?
+
Yes, 100%. We only sell genuine, original products from verified suppliers. All iPhones come sealed in box with original accessories and full Apple warranty support.
Do you deliver outside Harare?
+
Yes. We deliver nationwide across Zimbabwe including Bulawayo, Gweru, Mutare, Masvingo, and more. WhatsApp us to confirm delivery timeline and cost for your area.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept EcoCash, USD cash, bank transfer, ZiG, and Innbucks. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange payment for your order.
Do you offer warranty on products?
+
All products come with the manufacturer's standard warranty. Additionally, we offer support and assistance for any issues that arise after purchase. Message us on WhatsApp for warranty queries.
Can I trade in my old device?
+
Yes! We accept trade-ins on iPhones and Samsung Galaxy devices. Message us on WhatsApp with your device model and condition for a valuation.
